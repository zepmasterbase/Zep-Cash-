About Zep Cash (ZAC)

Zep Cash (ZAC) is the official utility token powering the student creator economy. Designed for university students, creators, and freelancers, ZAC enables instant tipping, rewards, and cross-border payments within our ecosystem.

Our Mission

  • Empower student creators globally
  • Reward music, podcast, and freelance work instantly
  • Create a seamless, decentralized student economy
  • Bridge global transactions using Base Network

Where Zep Cash Is Used

  • Campus Wave: Tip your favorite student artists and podcasters, earn rewards, and collaborate globally.
  • Zep Swipe: Student freelancers get paid instantly and securely in ZAC.
  • University Ecosystems: Build a digital economy for student creators and organizations.
